Sejal Software

Sejal Software offers consulting, project implementation, data migration and training services for Cloud (Salesforce.com CRM, Salesforce Platform (Force.com)), Mobile, and Social technologies.

Our consulting services offer reviewing your current business process, recommend best practices and map Salesforce CRM functionality to identify gaps to be completed by configuration and customization.

We provide services for corporations and non profits organizations.

Our methodology and approach

We review and use industry best practices as part of our solutions to meet your business needs.

We apply the skills and expertise of Salesforce certified professionals for solution development, configuration, customization and support phases.

We evaluate and recommend apps to meet your business needs.

 

Services & Training

Consulting

  • Review existing applications used for Sales, Marketing and Service/Support operations.
  • Recommend right Salesforce edition (Group, Professional, and Enterprise) for you organization.
  • Offer support for your existing Salesforce Organization.

Implementation

  • Vanilla or Basic Implementation
  • Advanced and/or Premium Implementation
  • Extension and modifications to your existing implementation

Migration

  • Salesforce org to org migration
  • Migration from legacy CRM to Salesforce CRM
  • Migration of applications to Force.com platform
  • Dynamics CRM to Salesforce CRM or vice versa

Training

  • End User (Customized to the role of the users)
  • Administration (ADM 201 and ADM 211)
